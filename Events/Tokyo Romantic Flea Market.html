Tokyo Romantic Flea Market (Shibuya and Skytree) April 10-11 April 17-18 April 24-2

A special event held in two different locations: Shibuya and Oshiage (also known as Skytree)

Tokyo Romantic Flea Market: Shibuya Peruse over 100 shops offering antiques and vintage items from Japan and afar alongside handicrafts, accessories, fine art, organic foods and plants. Held in the green surrounds of Shibuya Garden Tower’s gardens, the market offers a relaxed Sunday spot for browsing.

Tokyo Skytree Flea Market: Starting from April, 2021, this will be the sister event. Also known as “Skytree Nominoichi”, this new Tokyo flea market is expected to take place almost every weekend, on both Saturdays and Sundays
